Newly Added Artists + more

  • Elegant Mess

    Hailing from Tallinn, Estonia and drawing inspiration from music throughout the ages, Elegant Mess presents listeners with a coordinated cacophony of sound that dares you to classify it.

  • Worse Look The Better

    Worse Look The Better are led by brother/sister duo Alexi and Ana Kyrog whose keen musical talents enable them to make songs filled with distorted guitars and thrashing percussion sound like meticulous masterpieces.

  • Godfrey

    While Godfrey's music explores a wide array of topics ranging from serious to mundane to downright whimisical, the presence of punchy horns and rhythmic beats keep audiences up and dancing through it all. Simply put, Godfrey is a good time.

  • The Finer Things

    Often classified as post-rock, The Finer Things offer a unique take on the genre by blending traditional instruments with their own homebrew constructions, which they build with microcomputers such as the Raspberry Pi.
