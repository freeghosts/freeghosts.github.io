PRIVACY POLICY


Intro & Overview

This Privacy Policy for Freeghosts Design & Development ("Freeghosts", "we", "us," or "our") explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how that information is used, under what circumstances we share information, and the choices you can make about that information.

TL;DR: Freeghosts does not currently collect any personal data from users other than what information users provide when contacting us whether by submitting the Contact Form or by any other means, and we only use this information to fulfill our obligation to the users. However, our site relies on third-party services for some of its funcitonality and their privacy practices may differ from ours.

Your Data & Freeghosts

When visiting our site, Freeghosts or our server providers may collect and temporarily store technical data related to your visit, such as your IP address, the time and date of your visit, the type and version of your browser and operating system, and the type of device used. We collect this data to ensure the stability, functionality, and security of our website.

Freeghosts does not collect any personal data from users other than what information you choose to provide when contacting us via the Contact Form or by any other means. We only use data submitted by you in order to fulfill our obligation to you, such as in response to an inquiry or data removal request.

As such, we retain your data only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill our obligation to the you unless required by law to do otherwise.

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.
  • The necessity for the performance of a contract with you.
  • Compliance with a legal obligation.
  • Our legitimate interests, provided these do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.

Freeghosts uses reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We, therefore, cannot guarantee security against all threats.


Your Data & Third Parties

Freeghosts uses third-party services for some of its functionality. We do not share any user data with these third parties other than what is necessary to maintain the security and functionality of the site as described above.

Please note that we use a third-party service to process form data. While their privacy and security policies state that the form owner (i.e. Freeghosts) is the sole data controller and submitted data is protected in transit by TLS 1.2 or greater and stored encrypted using AES-256 encryption, their servers are located in the United States of America. Therefore, any data you submit to us via the Contact Form will be processed and stored in the U.S. More information can be found at:

  • https://kwesforms.com/privacy
  • https://kwesforms.com/data-security

If you would rather your information not be stored in the U.S., you may contact us by email at support@freeghosts.com. Our email servers are located in Europe and use zero-knowledge encryption.

Freeghosts is not responsible for the privacy or security of other sites you may visit through links on our site.


Cookies

Cookies are small text files created by a web server and placed on your computer by your web browser. Cookies are generally used to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information.

Freeghosts does not currently use cookies, however, some of the third-party services we use do; namely, CodePen and KwesForms.

You can manage cookies by adjusting your cookie preferences in your browser settings. Be advised that if you block cookies completely, some features on our site may not function as a result. For further information about cookies and how to manage them, visit allaboutcookies.org.


International Data Transfers

Freeghosts is currently located in the United States, as are most of the third-party services we use. Therefore, it is possible that your information, including any personal data you submit to us, may be transferred, stored, and processed on servers located in the U.S. By using our services, you agree to this transfer, storing, or processing. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

As stated earlier in this Privacy Policy, if you would prefer data you submit to us to be processed and stored in Europe, which has stronger data protection and privacy regulations than the U.S., you may contact us by email at support@freeghosts.com.


Your Data Protection Rights

As a user and data subject, you have the following rights:

The right to access: You have the right to request Freeghosts for copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request us to complete any information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data under certain conditions.

The right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through one of the methods mentioned below.


How to Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, you may contact us either through the Contact Form or by email at support@freeghosts.com.
